## Scoring Reference

**Formula:** `Oracle × 2 + Value × 2 + Safety + Effort = max 30`

| Dimension | Max | Definition |
|-----------|-----|-----------|
| Oracle | 5×2=10 | How unambiguous is success? 5 = external hard metric, 1 = model grades own output |
| Value | 5×2=10 | Business impact: lead flow, revenue, brand protection, operational continuity |
| Safety | 5 | How safe is failure? 5 = read-only, 1 = sends to clients without approval |
| Effort | 5 | How hard to build? 5 = reuses all existing infra, 1 = new integration required |

**Park threshold:** Score < 16 → parked (not worth building until higher-value loops are live)
