## Active Directives

### Cross-Platform
- **PRIMARY GOAL:** High-quality **seller leads** in Louisiana. Lead quality > lead volume.
- **PRIMARY MARKETS (priority order):** 1) Greater Baton Rouge, 2) Acadiana/Lafayette, 3) Metro New Orleans (incl. Northshore — St. Tammany is fragmented/underserved = opportunity).
- **BRAND:** Luxury, editorial, morningside.studio-tier aesthetic. Never generic/templated. This is our #1 differentiator — every competitor uses dated IDX templates.
- **BUDGET:** $1,000/mo total during test phase. Split ~$550 Meta / ~$450 Google once Google is live.
- **THE OPEN LANE (exploit aggressively):** In all 3 markets the "sell my house" SERP is owned by cash-buyer/iBuyer spam, NOT agents — and almost no traditional agent runs Google Search ads. Intercept this with a polished agent valuation offer.

### Google Ads
- **PREFER** high-intent seller keywords: "what is my home worth [city]", "sell my house [city]", "home value [city]", "[city] listing agent".
- **CONSTRAINT:** Search campaigns only at launch. Display Network OFF, Search Partners OFF.
- **CONSTRAINT:** Location targeting = "Presence: people IN location" (not "interested in"). BR metro first.
- **CONSTRAINT:** Match types start EXACT + PHRASE only. No BROAD until conversion data exists.
- **CONSTRAINT:** Tight keyword lists for high Quality Score. Do NOT chase "more views" via broad bidding.
- **AVOID:** Bidding on cash-buyer terms with pure "fast cash" intent that attract distressed/lowball-only sellers unless landing page reframes to "full-market value, no lowball."
- **AVOID:** Naming competitors in ad copy.
- **BIDDING:** Start Maximize Clicks w/ max CPC cap (~$6–8) for ~2 weeks → switch to Maximize Conversions once conversion tracking has 30+ conversions.
- **MANDATORY:** Run `research_keywords` (real CPC/volume) before building any Search campaign.
- **MANDATORY:** Add extensions after creation — 10+ sitelinks, 8+ callouts, structured snippets, call extension, location extension. Extensions = free visibility + Quality Score.
- **LANDING PAGE:** Always send Google seller traffic to a dedicated home-valuation page — NEVER the generic homepage (tanks conversion + Quality Score).
- **CONVERSION TRACKING:** Must be set up before optimizing for leads. Wire form submit + call to AIRE. Adspirer can't set conversion-action priority — do that manually in Google Ads UI.

### Meta Ads
- **CONSTRAINT (legal):** All real estate ads = HOUSING Special Ad Category. NO targeting by age, gender, ZIP <15mi, homeowner status, income, behaviors, or detailed demographics. Advantage+ Audience is BLOCKED for housing.
- **TARGETING:** Broad BR metro (15mi+ radius) + let conversion algorithm find sellers. Add a "Special Ad Audience" (modeled from past-client list) once a seed list exists.
- **OBJECTIVE:** OUTCOME_LEADS with Instant Form + qualifying questions (address, timeframe to sell, primary residence?) to filter quality.
- **HARD BLOCKER (open):** A Facebook Page must be linked to ad account 2535749013427641 before any lead-gen ad or lead form can exist.
- **BUDGET:** Concentrate ~$33/day into ONE ad set to escape learning phase — do not fragment a small budget across many ad sets.

### Reviews / Organic (non-ad, but strategic context)
- **#1 LEVER:** Review-generation engine. Target 50+ Google reviews in 6 months (out-signals most solo luxury competitors), 100+ in 12 months. Post-close ask sequence.
- **ORGANIC MOAT:** Programmatic neighborhood/zip/subdivision pages + monthly market-report blog (AIRE can generate at scale). Start with subdivisions where The W Group is thinnest.
